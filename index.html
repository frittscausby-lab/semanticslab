About

Fritts Causby writes about things worth paying attention to: landmark properties, financial markets, and the stories behind both. His work has appeared in Wrightsville Beach Magazine, on the website for Berkshire Hathaway HomeServices California Properties, for a regional law firm whose attorneys publish the content under their own names, and many others. His real estate pitches have informed features in Robb Report and Mansion Global.

Over two decades, Fritts has written property descriptions for residential and commercial real estate in the United States, Europe, and Mexico, and developed content for clients in finance, healthcare, real estate, and beyond. He publishes The Pinnacle, a newsletter covering luxury real estate, stock market insights, and the aspirational lifestyle, written from the perspective of someone who has learned both the hard way.

He holds a bachelor's degree in English from North Carolina State University and a master's degree in Human Resources and Organization Development from National University. Outside of work, he surfs, plays golf, and travels when possible.
